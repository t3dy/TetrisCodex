Alchemy Snake

Old-school grid Snake using shared alchemical blocks. Eat zodiac/principle matter, survive the consequences, and let the narration parser interpret every digestion.

Alchemy Rules

Salt fixes the body: slower pace, more growth, brief stability.
Sulphur burns: bonus score, tail loss, heat danger if repeated.
Mercury volatilizes: faster pace, ghost pass, slippery turns.
Apparatus tiles alter the next few digestions by process.
