New Historiography Of Alchemy

The arcade treats alchemy as a serious historical field: laboratory practice, natural philosophy, medicine, metallurgy, poetry, emblem books, patronage, and the long prehistory of chemistry. The games can exaggerate physics, but each challenge should point back toward real texts, people, operations, and theories.

Scholarly Values

serious play

Take Alchemists Seriously

The games model historical theories as playable rules without asking players to accept every claim literally.

procedural culture

Text, Image, Instrument

Recipes, poems, emblems, furnaces, vessels, and repeated operations become level frames and block mechanics.

ambiguity

Preserve Layers

Alchemy often works at practical, allegorical, spiritual, and secretive registers at the same time.

chemistry

Continuity And Difference

Alchemy connects to chemistry, medicine, mining, metallurgy, theology, poetry, and courtly knowledge.

Topic Areas

Tria Prima

Salt, Sulphur, and Mercury become fixed, oily/combustible, and volatile matter classes.

Seven Metals And Planets

Planet glyphs can map to metals, receivers, affinities, weights, and transformation goals.

Twelve Processes

Zodiac buttons name operations such as calcination, dissolution, distillation, separation, multiplication, and projection.

Apparatus Culture

Crucibles, alembics, athanors, molds, and vessels become furniture, hazards, and tools.

Emblems And Poetry

Atalanta-style image/text/music culture becomes tutorial structure, move feedback, and codex material.

Transmutation

Lead-to-gold levels dramatize the goal while explaining what is gameplay fiction.

Level Source Frame

Every historically framed level should show a compact side frame: challenge premise, historical idea, person or text, what is exaggerated for gameplay, and what to notice mechanically.
