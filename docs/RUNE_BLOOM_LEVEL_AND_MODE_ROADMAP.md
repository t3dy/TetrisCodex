# Rune Bloom Level and Mode Roadmap

## Current Mode: Garden

The shipped prototype is Garden Mode.

Rules:

- 9x9 board
- random glyphs
- four elements
- bloom runs of 4+
- blight every 3 turns
- win at 220 points

Purpose:

Teach the core loop and reveal whether the game is fun before adding systems.

## Campaign Structure

Suggested campaign: 5 chapters, 8 levels each.

### Chapter 1: Germination

Teaches:

- adjacency
- rotation
- bloom lines
- blight spread

Level ideas:

1. First Seed: no blight, score 60
2. Four in a Row: only two elements
3. Edge Wind: blight appears every 5 turns
4. Clean Cut: purify 3 blight cells
5. Square Garden: square pieces appear more often
6. Line Keeper: bar pieces appear more often
7. Tight Soil: 7x7 board variant
8. First Bloom Trial: score 180

### Chapter 2: The Four Elements

Teaches:

- element-specific effects
- color planning
- element goals

Level ideas:

1. Aqua Channels: Aqua blooms extend one cell
2. Ember Orchard: Ember blooms burn adjacent blight
3. Moss Return: Moss blooms regrow one rune
4. Star Paths: Star reads diagonals
5. Mixed Bed: all element powers active
6. Overgrowth: Moss can clog the board
7. Spark Hazard: Ember can burn friendly cells
8. Elemental Exam: score 240

### Chapter 3: Salt, Sulphur, Mercury

Teaches:

- Tria Prima modifiers
- catalysts
- persistent cell states

Level ideas:

1. Salt Anchor: protected cells
2. Sulphur Spark: controlled destruction
3. Mercury Wild: wildcard blooms
4. Fixed Gate: make a new seed
5. Volatile Line: Mercury shifts after bloom
6. Too Much Fire: Sulphur risk level
7. Salt Wall: seal edge blight
8. Three Principles: use all three to win

### Chapter 4: Zodiac Lab

Teaches:

- palette buttons
- two-click crafting
- process verbs

Level ideas:

1. Aries Furnace: calcination tutorial
2. Cancer Solvent: dissolution tutorial
3. Virgo Column: distillation tutorial
4. Libra Lift: sublimation tutorial
5. Aquarius Bloom: multiplication tutorial
6. Pisces Ray: projection tutorial
7. Compound Garden: combine process + principle
8. Lab Practical: craft 8 reactions

### Chapter 5: The Sealed Garden

Teaches:

- mastery
- high-pressure blight
- reaction chains

Level ideas:

1. White Blight: hardened blight
2. No Empty Edge: blight starts inside
3. Four Corners: multiple seeds
4. Withering Queue: piece queue shrinks
5. Mirror Rows: horizontal blooms only
6. Black Sun: Nigredo timer cells
7. Rubedo Bloom: final transformation
8. The Waking Garden: score 500

## Additional Modes

### Puzzle Mode

Hand-authored starting boards with exact objectives.

Examples:

- clear all blight in 6 moves
- score exactly 80
- create a Star bloom
- preserve all Salt cells

### Lab Mode

The player uses zodiac + Tria Prima buttons to craft pieces.

Core appeal:

Not "what piece did I get?" but "what tool do I make?"

### Draft Mode

Choose one of three pieces each turn.

Good for:

- lower randomness
- strategic play
- daily challenge seeds

### Siege Mode

Blight is more aggressive.

Parameters:

- blight every 2 turns
- blight prefers cells near runes
- hardened blight appears

### Zen Mode

No score pressure, no loss until board full.

Good for:

- visual pleasure
- learning
- mobile-friendly play

## Challenge Card Ideas

Borrowing the "challenge card" framing from the existing docs:

- No Ember blooms
- All blooms must touch the seed
- Win with 12 or fewer placements
- Purify 10 blight
- Never let blight touch the center
- Score only from chains
- Use every shape at least once
- Win with no Mercury
