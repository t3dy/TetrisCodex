# Inspirations: Puzzle Games for Alchemical Process Mechanics

This document collects puzzle-game inspirations for realizing alchemical processes as playable mechanics. The goal is not to clone these games, but to identify strong mechanical patterns that can become alchemical verbs: calcine, dissolve, fix, distill, separate, ferment, project, multiply, and so on.

## Core Spatial Puzzle Lineage

### Tetris

Useful mechanics:

- falling polyomino placement
- rotation under pressure
- line completion as transformation
- preview queue and bag randomness

Alchemical translation:

- line clears become **distillation** or **purification**
- awkward pieces become unstable matter
- the next-piece queue becomes the visible future of an experiment
- completed rows could transform into an extracted essence instead of disappearing

Best fit:

- Balance Tetris
- alchemical apparatus pieces
- matter sorted by weight, volatility, or planetary side

### Puyo Puyo

Useful mechanics:

- color grouping
- chain reactions
- garbage pressure
- small placements creating large delayed outcomes

Alchemical translation:

- same-trait groups become substances undergoing **conjunction**
- chain reactions model **fermentation** or **multiplication**
- garbage blobs become dross, slag, caput mortuum, or blight
- delayed cascades make the Great Work feel alive

Best fit:

- Rune Bloom-style reaction gardens
- alchemical chain scoring
- volatile Mercury setups

### Dr. Mario

Useful mechanics:

- matching to eliminate viruses
- capsules split across two colors
- board as infection field
- constraint from existing blockers

Alchemical translation:

- viruses become corruption, disease, blight, or putrefaction
- pills become paired reagents
- matching is **medicine**, **purification**, or **dissolution**
- two-color capsules map well to compound substances

Best fit:

- healing/purification levels
- disease of metals
- curing Saturn/lead into Sun/gold

### Columns

Useful mechanics:

- vertical falling triads
- cycling order within a piece
- matching in multiple directions
- jewel-like transformation

Alchemical translation:

- cycling a triad mirrors Salt/Sulphur/Mercury ordering
- vertical compounds can be volatile, fixed, or combustible depending on arrangement
- diagonal matches can be astral/zodiacal effects

Best fit:

- Tria Prima stack pieces
- zodiac-aligned color matching

### Lumines

Useful mechanics:

- time-sweeping clear line
- rhythm-based resolution
- completed areas wait until scanned

Alchemical translation:

- a furnace cycle or planetary hour sweeps the board
- reactions do not resolve instantly; they wait for the **operation phase**
- timing becomes part of distillation, digestion, and fermentation

Best fit:

- Athanor furnace mode
- timed laboratory cycles
- music/rhythm alchemy

## Physics and Balance Inspirations

### Tricky Towers

Useful mechanics:

- Tetris pieces with gravity
- unstable towers
- spells/powerups
- race, survival, and puzzle modes

Alchemical translation:

- spells become alchemical operations
- tower instability becomes volatility
- pieces can be fixed by Salt, burned by Sulphur, or liquefied by Mercury

Best fit:

- Balance Tetris
- platform tipping and profitable dumping
- laboratory spell buttons

### World of Goo

Useful mechanics:

- sticky nodes
- structural tension
- different goo types
- building toward a pipe/goal

Alchemical translation:

- Salt nodes fix structures
- Mercury nodes are fluid connectors
- Sulphur nodes burn or energize
- apparatus furniture can anchor, pull, or conduct reactions

Best fit:

- alchemical bridge/tower modes
- conjunction as literal structural binding

### Boom Blox

Useful mechanics:

- block physics
- targeted destruction
- chain collapses
- different block materials

Alchemical translation:

- Calcination destroys structure
- Separation removes a chosen link
- Incineration causes chain collapse
- heavy metals and light vapors behave differently

Best fit:

- destructive alchemy
- apparatus that intentionally sheds material off the platform

### Jenga

Useful mechanics:

- removing support pieces
- tension between extraction and collapse
- physical consequence from tiny changes

Alchemical translation:

- Distillation removes essence from the structure
- Separation extracts a cell while testing stability
- wrong extraction produces collapse, ash, or dross

Best fit:

- alchemical tower surgery
- balance-platform risk

## Design Rule

Every borrowed mechanic must become alchemical:

- not “bomb,” but **Calcination**
- not “wildcard,” but **Mercury**
- not “locked block,” but **Salt**
- not “multiplier,” but **Multiplication**
- not “line clear,” but **Distillation**
- not “chain combo,” but **Fermentation into Multiplication**

The inspiration is mechanical. The identity should remain alchemical.
