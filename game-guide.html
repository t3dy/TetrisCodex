Alchemical Game World Guide

This arcade is a family of physics puzzles built from emoji blocks, alchemical properties, strange platforms, scholar-sourced motifs, and creator tools. The games share assets and rules, but each mode asks the player to use balance, motion, revelation, transformation, or construction differently.

Game Modes

⚖️🜍

Balance Tetris

main branch

Stack tetromino-like matter on a tilting platform. In alchemy variants, profit comes from tipping the correct substances off the correct side.

♈☿

Alchemy Palette

world grammar

Zodiac process buttons and Salt/Sulphur/Mercury modifiers create apparatus tiles, fixed substances, combustible oils, and volatile matter.

🐕📜

Wild Hunt

legend mode

Hounds, hoofbeats, apparitions, wind, date tokens, and chronicle scoring reshape Balance Tetris into a folklore-driven campaign.

🚕✨

VALIS Space Taxi

vehicle mode

Pilot a craft through variable gravity, land on tilting alchemical pads, reveal hidden cargo with a beam, and prevent lab cascades.

🌉🐎

Moat Drawbridge

puzzle branch

Build a stack tall and flat enough to become a crossing for horses, coaches, and royal cargo after the bridge tips over the moat.

✶🌿

Rune Bloom

separate experiment

A crystalline tile-placement garden with glyph pieces, bloom clearing, blight spread, and many fast mode variants.
