Alchemical Game Arcade

A workbench for alchemical block games. The priority branch is Balance Tetris with a zodiac and Tria Prima palette: click a process sign for apparatus furniture, or add Salt, Sulphur, or Mercury to create fixed, combustible, or volatile substances.

⚖️🜍

Balance Tetris

priority branch

Stack tetromino-like matter on a tilting platform. In Alchemy mode, profit by tipping planetary substances off their proper side.
